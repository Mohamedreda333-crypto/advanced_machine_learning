### Problem Statement
Plant diseases cause significant economic losses in agriculture worldwide. Traditional methods of disease identification are:
- Time-consuming and labor-intensive
- Require expert knowledge
- Often lead to delayed treatment
- Result in reduced crop yields and quality

### Proposed Solution
Our AI-driven solution addresses these challenges by:
- Providing instant disease identification from leaf images
- Offering 24/7 accessibility through web and mobile platforms
- Delivering accurate diagnosis with treatment recommendations
- Enabling early intervention to prevent disease spread
